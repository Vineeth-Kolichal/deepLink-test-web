Kettle Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Monday, 1st September 2025

Welcome to Kettle. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app or website.

By using Kettle, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Gender
  • Age/Date of Birth
  • Profile photo(s)
  • Bio, profession, and interests
  • Email address and/or phone number
  • Your preferences (who you want to connect with)

b. Automatically Collected

We may also collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, device ID)
  • IP address and location (if you grant permission)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent)
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

c. Optional Inputs

  • Photos or media uploaded voluntarily
  • Location for nearby discovery features
  • Contact syncing (with permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with relevant people
  • Show users nearby or based on filters
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Communicate updates or promotional content (optional)
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our terms

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Other users (based on your privacy settings)
  • Service providers who help us run the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Law enforcement, only if legally required
  • Third parties, only in the case of a merger or acquisition

4. Your Choices and Controls

You can:

  • Edit or delete your profile at any time
  • Control location or notification access via your device settings
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy or deletion of your data (email us at support@kettleapp.com)

5. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • For up to 90 days after account deletion (for legal/audit purposes)
  • Anonymized data may be stored for analysis

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies or similar tools to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember preferences
  • Understand how users engage with the app
  • Improve overall experience

You can disable cookies in your browser/app settings, though some features may not function properly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Kettle is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data using:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure servers
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. International Users

If you're accessing Kettle from outside India, please note that your data will be processed and stored in India and may be subject to Indian laws.

10. Your Rights Under Law

Depending on your region, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

We will honor these rights in accordance with applicable laws.

11. Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India, and any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Kochi.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. You’ll be notified if major changes are made. Continued use of Kettle means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

Got questions or privacy concerns? Email us at support@kettleweb.com
